    <p class="home-card__eyebrow">Current focus</p>
    <h2>Agentic Engineering (AE+X)</h2>
    <p>
      I treat AI systems as workflows that must be measured, audited, and improved — not just demoed. My core question is how to make multi-agent systems deliver <strong>reliable outcomes</strong> in real research and production settings.
    </p>
    <ul class="home-list">
      <li>LLM-assisted qualitative coding, calibration, and failure analysis.</li>
      <li>Evidence-grounded generation and traceability for high-stakes AI use.</li>
      <li>Code-first media workflows spanning scripting, visuals, packaging, and publishing.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="home-card">
    <p class="home-card__eyebrow">What I build</p>
    <ul class="home-list home-list--tight">
      <li>Evaluation harnesses, datasets, and QA loops for human/LLM hybrid workflows.</li>
      <li>RAG and tool-using agent systems designed for reproducibility rather than demos.</li>
      <li>Teaching and studio formats that connect prototyping, documentation, and responsible AI practice.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
