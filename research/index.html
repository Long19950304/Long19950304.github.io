Research

Research directions and selected projects (AE+X).

My research program centers on Agentic Engineering (AE+X): building and evaluating multi-agent systems that deliver reliable outcomes in applied communication and digital media contexts.

Agentic Engineering (AE+X)

A unifying research agenda for configuring, testing, and deploying multi-agent systems for reliable outcomes.

Reliable LLM-Assisted Qualitative Analysis

Benchmarks, calibration, and QA for human/LLM hybrid coding in communication research.

Computational Social Science & Digital Methods

Computational communication research using large-scale digital trace data, measurement, and causal designs.

AI-Native Media Pipelines

Code-first multi-model pipelines for scripting → visuals → subtitles → packaging → publishing (with evaluation and QA).
